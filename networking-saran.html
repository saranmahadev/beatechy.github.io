Networking

When two devices are connected and shares information between them then it is called as Networking. In Simple words, When two friends share information between them then they are connected in Network. When you have Girl or Boy as your friend but you always refer them as friend. In the same way when any computer or printer or anything is connected to a network then it is called as Node.

Protocol

When we share information between friends, we might have some secrets and we do not share that secret. To keep that secret we will develop certain Rules to not share the information that Rules is called as Protocol. We have the protocol to not to share the information. In the same way, When we have two computers are connected, they follow certain protocols to communicate between them.

Types of Networking



A Computer Network is Mainly of four types:

I. LAN(Local Area Network)
II. PAN(Personal Area Network)
III. MAN(Metropolitan Area Network)
IV. WAN(Wide Area Network)

Local Area Network


When two or more nodes are connected with cables or wirelessly within a Building or Locally, then Network is called as Local Area Network.

Personal Area Network

When two or more nodes are connected with cables or wirelessly within a Personal space, then Network is called as Personal Area Network.

Metropolitan Area Network

When two or more nodes are connected with cables or wirelessly within a small Geographical Area, then Network is called as Metropolitan Area Network.

Wide Area Network

When two or more nodes are connected with cables or wirelessly within a larger Geographical Area, then Network is called as Wide Area Network.

Components

There are many Components in the Network. We can divide the Components into two:

# Hardware Components

and

# Software Components



Hardware Components include:

# Routers

# Firewalls
# etc...

Software Components include:
