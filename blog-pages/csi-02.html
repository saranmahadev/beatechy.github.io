Ising Model
August 13, 2020 by Dr.S.Jeya

It is a mathematical model designed to explain various phenomenon exhibited by interacting systems. For example, phenomenon of ferromagnetism, anti ferromagnetism, melting of ice and various phase transition can be analyzed using Ising model.
