Know Some AI Basics

In Today’s world, AI is like a fancy thing which most of them are attracted to learn and it is perfect field to go today but first understand some basic things before entering AI. AI is a technique that enables machines to mimic human behavior and for adapting to human behaviour which is trained using neural networks.

Neural network is a name many people who are in tech field would have heard but many may not. Let me explain you about neural networks in a very strange way. You and me are human beings who always make mistakes. So, let us consider a mistake, you went into a car and when you closed the door without noticing that your other hand is on the closing area, you will get hurt. After that incident you will not close the door until your hands are away from the closing area. So, what happed here? You learnt from your mistakes and you changed yourself according to the desired results.

Let’s explain this process more in a technical way.
• You Entered a Car which is the Input
• You got hurt which is the output
• Brain alters itself to check your hand whenever you enter a car again

Neural Networks also works in the same way as the brain does, you give an input into a neural network and it gives an output and the output will get a feedback which may be positive or negative. If the feedback is negative then the neural networks alters itself and runs the process again and again until it gets a positive feedback which is obviously called as Machine Learning. Deep Neural Networks are applied to run unsupervised learning tasks which leads to the development of Super Intelligence AI. Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence are two differnt topics,It is not necessary to learn Machine Learning first to learn Artificial Intelligence. If you are interested in Machine Learning, you can directly start with ML. If you are interested in implementing Computer vision and Natural Language Processing applications, you can directly start with AI.
